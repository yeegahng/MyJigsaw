Objective of MyJigsaw

Yes, we all know that there are tons of Android apps for Jigsaw puzzle, including freewares. They're even accessible very easily. Here I would like to build up a totally free - without risk of payment by mistake - Jigsaw puzzle app. Actually my kid love to play Jigsaw puzzle on the Android device. I occationally notice him get disappointed when he face the payment menu in certain way. Most of the extended functions with full feature are not free. I, as an engineer with knowledge on Android platform, decided to make a toy for him - letting him free from such a frustration. Speaking about the motivation, this is it.
